Instructions for Use
  • Selecting a site(s) on the map will highlight all the sites within the subbasin, including the selected site(s). However, only details on the selected sites will be displayed in both the Monitoring Time Frames and Additional Information dashboards. Use the Map Toolbar or Ctrl+select to select more than one site. To reset the view, click anywhere on the map.
  • The drop-down filters in this dashboard also filter the Monitoring Time Frames and Additional Information dashboards.
  • Enable the "Highlight Selected Items" tool next to each legend header to select organizations or a range of monitoring years from the legends. The tool will only highlight sites on the map. Use Ctrl+select to select more than one Legend item. To reset, click anywhere on the map.
